About Me

I'm a passionate software developer who loves seeing my imaginations come to life. I like to take the things I enjoy from my hobbies and explore it more with coding! Whether it be leaving reviews to my favorite manga, exploring the world of tabletop games, or solving the most brain-moving puzzles, I love finding the innerworkings of things and transforming it with code. I have great experience working with NodeJS, React, Redux, and JavaScript. To me, the greatest pleasure in software development is being able to evolve a thought into a full fledged product that would make an impact to the world.
